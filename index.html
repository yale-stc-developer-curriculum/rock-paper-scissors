BUTTON

Pushing Your Buttons

Competing for almost 20 years, Team Button has proven to be one of the most prominent teams in the Rock, Paper, Scissors Competition. They rank #1 in the HTML Conference and are quickly climbing up the ranks nationally.

Hailing from the small town of Tewksbury, MA, Dana Chaykovsky is considered one of the fiercest forces on this side of the Mississippi. She sails across the ocean and fights space cowboys along with her rock, paper, scissors endeavors and is also a world-renowned connoisseur of sweets.

A beautiful woman from colorful Argentina, Natalia Castillejo has been training for rock, paper, scissors competitions since the age of eight. She trained under the most influential rock, paper, scissors champions of South America and now competes internationally in the sport. In her free time, she frequently swims across the English Channel and presents her painitings to the New York Metropolitan Museum.
